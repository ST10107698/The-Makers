About Us

­­­The most authentic, leading retailer of sporting goods in South Africa

Mention Sportsmans Warehouse to a South African, and they'll tell you a childhood memory. Our journey into the life of South African households began in 1986, when the first Sportsmans Warehouse opened its doors in Rondebosch, Cape Town.

As the first sporting goods superstore of its kind in South Africa, we have since established a formidable footprint, with 49 stores across all 9 provinces in Southern Africa - including Namibia and our online store.
